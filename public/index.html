Now's the time for a new PC

Windows 8.1 was designed to power an exciting and affordable new generation of tablets and PCs. You'll be amazed by what a computer can do now.Power up your new Windows tablet or PC and you can get into the flow right away. The key is to explore the corners and edges, where you'll find most of the things you do most often, like switching between apps, sharing things with friends, and adjusting your settings. You'll soon be zooming along from work to play and back again—or doing both at once.New tablets are versatile and stylish, blazing fast and ultra light. Laptops and 2‑in‑1 PCs with snap-in keyboards give you speed and power with complete portability. All-in-one PCs and desktops offer bigger displays for both serious productivity and serious play.

Mouse, keyboard, and familiar programs

Get a new touchscreen PC and you can still work with a mouse, keyboard, and the Windows desktop the way you always have. Use virtually any printer. Programs that work on Windows 7 work on Windows 8.1. All the basics, from startup times to security, are better than ever.

Touchscreens, apps, and Bing Smart Search

With apps from the Windows Store, and new ways to tap, swipe, and zoom around, Windows 8.1 gives you a world of touchscreen entertainment, and a better way to find what you need with Bing Smart Search. And OneDrive—free online storage that's built into Windows 8.1—means your files are always with you, from any of your PCs or devices.
